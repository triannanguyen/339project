What are Visitors?

Visitors are animals who will visit your city/town/village/island occasionally. Some visitors will come weekly and some will only come on special occasions! When they come they're usually selling something or theres a special event going on. I'll be rating my top 9 favorite visitors!

1. Jingle

Jingle comes on Christmas to give gifts.

2. Daisy Mae

Daisy Mae comes on Sundays to sell turnips.

3. K.K. Slider

K.K. Slider comes on Saturdays to perform songs.

4. Saharah

Saharah comes to sell rugs/flooring/wallpaper.

5. Celeste

Celeste comes to stargaze.

6. Resetti

Resetti comes to yell at you when you reset.

7. Flick

Flick comes to buy bugs.

8. Gulliver

Gulliver comes as a lost seagull who needs help getting back to his ship.

9. Wisp

Wisp comes as a ghost who needs help collecting his spirits.
